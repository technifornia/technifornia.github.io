Our Story

Technifornia began when Aaron was doing a second Bachelors in Computer Science and the classwork wasn't practical; the web programming class wouldn't teach the latest languages because of liscensing costs. That led us on the path of finding something better - courses that prepare you to work! Aaron completed a course at Tech Talent South and over time became their lead Instructor, and curriculum designer for every region.

We are bringing our proven success back home to SoCal. Whether you want to add to your skills running your business or switch careers to work full-time in web dev, we will mentor and guide you on your journey.
